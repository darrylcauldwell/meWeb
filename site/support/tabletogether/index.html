About TableTogether

TableTogether is a family meal planning app that helps you organize recipes, plan meals, and create shopping lists. Share recipes and meal plans with your family members using iCloud sharing.

Getting Started

Adding Recipes

Tap the + button to add a new recipe. Enter the recipe name, ingredients, and instructions. You can also add a photo from your camera or photo library.

Planning Meals

Navigate to the Meal Plan tab to assign recipes to specific days. Swipe left or right to change weeks.

Shopping Lists

Shopping lists are automatically generated from your meal plan, or you can create custom lists manually.

Family Sharing

Share your recipes and meal plans with family members by inviting them through the app's sharing feature. They'll need to have TableTogether installed and be signed into iCloud.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why aren't my recipes syncing?

Make sure you're signed into iCloud on all your devices and that iCloud Drive is enabled. Check Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > iCloud Drive.

Can I use TableTogether offline?

Yes! Your recipes and meal plans are cached locally. Changes will sync when you're back online.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual recipes, meal plans, or shopping lists within the app. To remove all data, delete the app and manage your iCloud storage through Settings.
